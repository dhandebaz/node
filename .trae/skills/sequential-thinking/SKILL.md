# Sequential Thinking

This skill enables sequential thinking and problem decomposition.

## Usage

Use this skill when you need to:
- Break down complex problems into manageable steps
- Analyze a situation thoroughly before acting
- Perform deep reasoning or "think step by step"
- Review your own thought process or previous actions

## How it works

The skill provides tools to structure your thinking process, allowing you to maintain a chain of thought that can be reviewed and refined.
